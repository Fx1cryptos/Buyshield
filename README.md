# 🛡️ BuyShield

> **Buy with Confidence. Every Time.**

BuyShield is a secure escrow marketplace platform that protects buyers and sellers during online transactions. Instead of sending money directly to a seller, payments are securely held by BuyShield until the buyer confirms that the correct item has been received in the expected condition.

Our mission is to eliminate online shopping scams and build trust in digital commerce.

# ❌ The Problem

Millions of people lose money every year because of online scams.

Common problems include:

- Paying for products that never arrive
- Receiving fake or damaged products
- Sellers dealing with fraudulent buyers
- Lack of trust when buying from social media
- No protection for peer-to-peer transactions
- Difficult dispute resolution

---

# ✅ Our Solution

BuyShield acts as a trusted third party between buyers and sellers.

Instead of paying the seller directly:

1. Buyer creates a protected transaction.
2. Buyer deposits payment into BuyShield.
3. Seller ships the item.
4. Buyer receives and inspects the product.
5. BuyShield releases payment after buyer confirmation.
6. If something goes wrong, BuyShield manages the dispute.
